Assignment: Document Intelligence Pipeline for Legal Analytics
Scenario:
You are assisting a legal firm in automating the ingestion and analysis of diverse documents including invoices, contracts, meeting minutes, and emails. All are provided in PDF format.
Objective:
The firm seeks to conduct a Proof of Concept (POC) to digitize and streamline its data ingestion and organization process. The goal is to extract metadata and key information from documents for subsequent analytics and retrieval activities (e.g., reporting, search, summarization, and aggregation).

Show how the pipeline is modular and scalable (e.g., ingestion, classification, extraction, transformation, storage).
Explain how the extracted data can be used for downstream applications such as reporting, search, and summarization.
 
2. Python Prototype (LLM Workflow)
Develop a Python-based LLM workflow that:
Ingests and processes PDF documents.
Classifies them by type (e.g., contract, invoice, email).
Extracts structured fields such as:
Client Name
Invoice Amount
Date(s)
Involved Parties
You are responsible for defining the data schema/structure for the extracted information.
The focus is on demonstrating the core workflow logic, not building a production-grade system.
